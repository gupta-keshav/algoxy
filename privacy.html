Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Algoxy AI ("we," "our," "us") is an AI engineering consultancy operating the website at algoxy.ai. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@algoxy.ai.

Information We Collect

We collect information only in two ways:

  • Analytics data (with your consent) - pages visited, session duration, referrer URL, browser and device type, and approximate geographic location derived from your IP address, collected via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity.
  • Contact information - if you email us directly, we receive your email address and any details you choose to share.

We do not collect personal information through any forms on this website.

Cookies & Tracking

We use tracking technologies only with your consent. If you click "Accept" on our cookie banner:

  • Google Analytics 4 sets cookies to distinguish visitors and measure sessions.
  • Microsoft Clarity sets cookies for anonymized session recording and heatmaps.

Your consent choice is stored in your browser's localStorage (key: algoxy-consent). No personal data is stored there. If you decline, no tracking cookies are set. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing site data for algoxy.ai in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how visitors interact with our site and improve its content and performance.
  • To respond to business inquiries sent via email.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except as described below.

Third-Party Services

When you accept cookies, data is shared with:

  • Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) - governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity (Microsoft Corporation) - governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Data processed by these services may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your country.

Data Retention

  • Google Analytics 4: usage data is retained for 14 months (Google's default).
  • Microsoft Clarity: session data is retained for 13 months.
  • Contact emails: retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To submit a request, email us at contact@algoxy.ai. If you are located in the EU/EEA or UK, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the site following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, reach us at contact@algoxy.ai.
